Cook to survive!

Cook to survive from a horde of zombies by feeding their hunger!

Zombies from every direction will come towards your food truck demanding food until they are satisfied...or else your life depends on it! Use your cooking abilities to make burgers, fries, and drinks for the hungry undead.

Mastering your culinary skills and perfecting your throws will be key to your survival.

Survive longer by equipping power-ups! These abilities include cooking faster, better defense, magnetic food and many more! Mix and match up to three power-ups to your liking to survive and get a higher score!

Decorate your food truck with different wallpapers, floorings and detailing!

Change your perspective by changing the game's graphics!

Brag about your earned achievements and leaderboard ranks to your friends!
