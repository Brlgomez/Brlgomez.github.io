Game Maker Language

Team Size: 2

Role: Lead game designer, programmer and artist

A 2D platformer with a twist where you cannot be in direct sunlight. I programmed the player, enemies, and a way the game can tell if the player is underneath the sun. Also, the game takes in consideration if the player built a structure over themselves to protect from the sunlight. I also created the art direction, sprites and level design.
