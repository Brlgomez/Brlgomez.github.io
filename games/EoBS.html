Eye on Baby Sequoia (PC, November 2015)

Language: C# in Unity

Team Size: 1

Roles: The lead programmer, game designer, artist

An ambient tree growing interactive art where the player will progress the growth of their Sequoia. The player experience oldness from the mass amount of life witnesses, from the passages and from the complete change in the tree and environment.
