Hello, my name is Brandon Gomez, a senior computer game designer attending the University of California, Santa Cruz. I have always been interested in video games since I was an adolescent. I would watch my brothers play Super Mario World and I would get the sense of adrenaline and fear from watching my brothers beat the final boss. It was such an emotional rush that I would only feel in this particular medium. Games such as Halo, Shadow of the Colossus, Portal, Thirty Flights of Loving, The Last of Us and Gone Home helped me begin to see videogames as an art form. These games would inspire me to create games that are unconventional, beautiful, and impactful.
